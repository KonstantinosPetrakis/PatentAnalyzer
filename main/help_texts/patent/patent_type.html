Parent types :
  • Utility: A patent for a new and useful process, machine, manufacture, or composition of matter, or a new and useful improvement thereof. This is the most common type of patent and what typically comes to mind when people think of a patent.
  • Design: A patent for a new, original, and ornamental design for a product.
  • Plant: A patent for a new and distinct variety of plant.
  • Reissue: A patent for a new and useful process, machine, manufacture, or composition of matter, or a new and useful improvement thereof that has been reissued.
  • Defensive Publication A defensive publication is used to prevent another party from obtaining a patent on a product, apparatus or method for instance.
