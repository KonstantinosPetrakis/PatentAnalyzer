WIPO kinds (IPC):
  • A: Human Necessities
  • B: performing operations; transporting
  • C: Chemistry; Metallurgy
  • D: Textiles; Paper
  • E: Fixed Constructions
  • F: Mechanical Engineering; Lighting; Heating; Weapons; Blasting Engines or Pumps
  • G: Physics
  • H: Electricity
* Each of the above groups is further divided into subgroups that are divided into sub-subgroups and so on. For example, group A has a subgroup A01, which has a sub-subgroup A01B, which has a sub-sub-subgroup A01B1/00.
